THE HISTORY OF A D U M B R A T E

4TH OF MARCH, 2016

LAUNCHING OF ADUMBRATE

The issue over bodies of water have finally reached the head of United Nations, who announced that the said organization will be launching ADUMBRATE, not only to give a better meaning to the word but to save the dying waters of the world.

The first branch of the ADUMBRATE project has been launched in Madison, Wisconsin on the 1st of May, 2016. The first-appointed and current head admin of the Madison, Wisconsin Branch of ADUMBRATE is June Vwj, a Hmong coming from Oshkosh.
