A D U M B R A T E

ADUMBRATE is a program of United Nations which aims to restore the bodies of water back to beautiful and resourceful fjords. It aims to give another meaning to the word ADUMBRATE, which originally means to sketch out vaguely. The meaning of ADUMBRATE issued by United Nations is to stand up from ignorance to restore the bodies of water to what they once were.
